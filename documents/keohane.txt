Keohane 4E is one of the most up to date dorms on campus and has large rooms and floor amenities like a large common room and kitchen on the 1st and 3rd floor and printing stations. Large windows to let in a lot of natural light and the views on the second floor and above can be quite nice. Very close to Pitchforks and other dorms like Hollows and Edens. Only complaint is that sometimes the AC feels like its circulating pretty stale air. Overall, great dorm and would live there again if I have the chance!

Keohane 4E should be judged separately from 4A, 4B, and 4D. I lived in 4B, which was a good experience. The rooms are pretty spacey, you have central AC, the bathrooms are big, and the building itself is nice (plus you get to see Peaches and Mamabean each time you walk to your room). Its location isn't as good as Few but much better than Edens, so it's neither a plus nor a minus. I'd definitely recommend Keohane but Keohane 4E is definitely the go-to if you can get it.
